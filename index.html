ABOUT US

Established in 1996, the IEEE Student Branch at the College of Engineering Chengannur (IEEE SB CEC) stands out as one of the most active and vibrant student branches in the country. Through a series of workshops, seminars, and technical events, the Student Branch empowers students to emerge as future leaders in their respective fields by connecting them with industry professionals, facilitating internships, and engaging in real-world projects that provide practical skills and experiences to complement their academic learning. Committed to engaging with the local community and promoting STEM education initiatives, IEEE SB CEC also benefits from the guidance and support of a dedicated team of mentors, comprising experienced professionals and faculty members. In essence, IEEE SB CEC is a testament to the power of collaboration, innovation, and education.

WHAT IS ARCANE?

Arcane is a 24-hour hackathon by IEEE SB, College of Engineering Chengannur, happening on 26th and 27th October across Trivandrum, Kochi, and Calicut. Participants will compete in three rounds: idea submission, a PPT round, and the final 24-hour offline hackathon. Teams of up to four members can join to innovate, collaborate, and bring their ideas to life.
